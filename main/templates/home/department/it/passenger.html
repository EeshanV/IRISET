Description:

The Lab is equipped with IPIS rev 2.0 of RDSO/SPN/TC/61.and IPIS rev 4.0 Integrated Passenger Information system with complete model setup along with application software & hardware like Platform display boards, Coach guidance display boards & At a glance display boards is established with hot standby mode.

Aimed to impart training to the trainees on the following topics.
  • Study of Passenger Information System
  • Display of Train information
  • Addition/Deletion To/From Train List
  • Audio Announcements over IPIS Network
  • PIS Network Configuration
  • Troubleshooting of IPIS Network
