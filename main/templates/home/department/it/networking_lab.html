Description:
This lab is aimed to train the trainees in concepts of TCP/IP networking by providing the real time environment. It is equipped with layer2 and layer3 switches, IP routers, SHDSL modems, Statmuxes and Terminal server. 

Training is imparted in the following topics: 
  • Establishing LAN, IP configuration of network devices
  • Establishing WAN, Configuration of Routers Cisco and Juniper with concepts of static and dynamic routing protocols
  • UTS/PRS network simulation
  • Analysis of LAN with LAN analyzer
  • Establishing last mile connectivity with SHDSL modems (Service provider location to Customer premises)
